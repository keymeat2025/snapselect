5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Photo Retention Periods

SnapSelect operates an automatic gallery deletion system based on plan validity periods:

  • Mini Plan: Photos retained for 7 days after plan expiry, then permanently deleted
  • Basic Plan: Photos retained for 14 days after plan expiry, then permanently deleted
  • Pro Plan: Photos retained for 14 days after plan expiry, then permanently deleted
  • Premium Plan: Photos retained for 21 days after plan expiry, then permanently deleted
  • Ultimate Plan: Photos retained for 21 days after plan expiry, then permanently deleted

5.2 Automatic Deletion Process

How the System Works:

  • Active Plans: Photos and galleries remain safe with no deletion risk
  • Plan Expiry: Automatic deletion system is immediately activated
  • Grace Period: Photos remain accessible during the retention period specified for your plan
  • Final Deletion: After grace period expires, all photos and galleries are permanently deleted without possibility of recovery
  • Plan Renewal: Renewing before or during grace period immediately stops the deletion process
