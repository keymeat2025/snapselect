2. Description of Service

SnapSelect is a photo workflow management platform that connects photographers with their clients. Our Service provides:

  • For Photographers: Tools to upload, organize, and share photo collections with clients
  • For Clients: Interface to view, select, and rate photos from their photography sessions
  • For Administrators: Platform management and oversight capabilities

The platform streamlines the photo selection process, eliminating email chains and enabling photographers to focus editing efforts on client-selected images.
